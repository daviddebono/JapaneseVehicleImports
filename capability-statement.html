Capability Statement

Japanese Vehicle Imports — We import Japanese cars, vans and trucks that are compliant in Australia. We manage all compliance and certification to get your vehicle here. We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart.

What We Do

We act as your broker for importing vehicles from Japan to Australia. We ensure each vehicle can be imported to Australia and manage compliance and certification from source to delivery. We have team members on the ground in Japan who inspect vehicles and purchase from auction on your behalf. We also offer bulk vehicle imports for dealerships: give us a shopping list, we confirm eligibility and quantity and manage the full process.

Who We Service

Consumers looking to import a car, van or truck, and car dealerships or fleets that need bulk or regular imports from Japan. We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart and coordinate delivery to your chosen port or location.

Locations
