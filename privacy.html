Privacy Policy

How we collect and use your information.

Japanese Vehicle Imports (“we”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information collected through this website.

Information We Collect

When you submit the enquiry form, we collect the details you provide: name, company, role, email, phone (optional), budget range (optional) and message. When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email. Form submissions are processed by a third-party form provider (e.g. Formspree) and forwarded to us; we do not display your email on the site.

How We Use It

We use enquiry data to respond to your request and provide our services. We use newsletter email addresses to send updates and promotions; you can unsubscribe at any time. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Retention and Security

We retain enquiry and newsletter data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. We take reasonable steps to protect your data; form data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Your Rights

You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us via the contact form. Australian Privacy Principle requests will be handled in line with applicable law.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page.

Contact us with any privacy questions.
