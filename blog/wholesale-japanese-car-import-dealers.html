Wholesale Japanese Car Import for Dealers

How dealers can use wholesale-style bulk imports: shopping lists, auction sourcing and full compliance with a team on the ground in Japan.

Summary

Dealers can give us a shopping list of desired Japanese cars. We confirm eligibility for Australia and the quantity we can source, then our team in Japan inspects and purchases from auction on your behalf. We manage full compliance and certification and deliver to your chosen location. One point of contact, consistent documentation. We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart.

Wholesale-Style Bulk Imports

For dealers, ordering multiple cars at once is more efficient than one-off imports. We treat your list as a bulk order: we confirm which models can be imported to Australia and in what quantity, then source them (including at Japanese auction where our team inspects and buys on your behalf), manage compliance and certification for each vehicle, and coordinate delivery. You get one broker, one process and consistent paperwork for the whole order.
