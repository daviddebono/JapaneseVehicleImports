Summary

Japanese vans (commercial and passenger) are popular in Australia for reliability and value. Importing one requires eligibility checks, compliance and certification. A broker ensures the vehicle can be imported to Australia and manages the full process. We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart and have team on the ground in Japan to inspect and purchase from auction.

Why Import a Japanese Van?

Japanese vans offer a strong combination of build quality, reliability and value. Businesses and fleets often use them for deliveries, trades and passenger use. Importing from Japan gives access to a wider range of models and specifications than may be available locally. The key is to use a broker who confirms Australian eligibility and manages compliance and certification so the van can be registered here.

Compliance and Certification

As with cars and trucks, Japanese vans must meet Australian design rules and may need to go through a RAWS-approved workshop for certification. Our Japanese van imports service handles this: we confirm the van can be imported to Australia, source it (including via auction where our team in Japan inspects and purchases on your behalf), and manage all compliance and delivery. We also do bulk van imports for businesses that need multiple units.

Where We Deliver

We coordinate delivery to your chosen port or location. We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart. Registration is then handled according to your state or territory requirements.
