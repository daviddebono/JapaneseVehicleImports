Japanese Truck Import Australia: Light and Medium Trucks

What trades and fleets need to know about importing light and medium trucks from Japan — eligibility, compliance and broker support.

Summary

Japanese light and medium trucks are in strong demand in Australia. Importing one requires eligibility verification, compliance and certification (e.g. RAWS where applicable). A broker confirms the truck can be imported to Australia and manages the full process. We have team on the ground in Japan who inspect and purchase from auction on your behalf. We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart.

Why Import a Japanese Truck?

Tradespeople and fleets often choose Japanese trucks for durability and value. The Japanese market offers a wide range of light and medium trucks that can be imported to Australia subject to eligibility and compliance. As your broker we check each model for Australian compliance, source the vehicle (including at auction where our team in Japan inspects and buys on your behalf), and manage certification and delivery.

Eligibility and Compliance

Not every Japanese truck can be imported. Eligibility depends on the model and Australian design rules. Our Japanese truck imports service includes an eligibility check before you commit, then full compliance and certification coordination. We also do bulk truck imports for dealerships or fleets that need multiple units — give us a list and we confirm eligibility and quantity.

Where We Service

We coordinate delivery to your chosen Australian port or location. We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart. Registration is handled according to your state or territory.

FAQ

Do you source trucks from Japanese auctions?

Yes. Our team in Japan inspects and purchases from auction on your behalf for trucks as well as cars and vans.

Can I import more than one truck?

Yes. We do bulk vehicle imports; give us a shopping list and we confirm eligibility and quantity and manage the full process.
