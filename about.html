About Us

Importing Japanese vehicles to Australia — with full compliance and a team on the ground in Japan.

Japanese Vehicle Imports helps consumers and dealerships import Japanese cars, vans and trucks to Australia. We only import vehicles that are compliant in Australia, and we manage all compliance and certification so your vehicle can be imported and registered here.

We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart. Whether you are a private buyer or a dealership with a shopping list of desired vehicles, we confirm eligibility and manage the full process from Japan to delivery.

Our Difference

Unlike many brokers, we have team members on the ground in Japan. They inspect vehicles and purchase from auction on your behalf, giving you better control over condition and price. For dealerships, we offer bulk vehicle imports: give us your list, we confirm eligibility and quantity and coordinate the rest.

What We Offer

We provide Japanese car imports, Japanese van imports and Japanese truck imports. Every vehicle is checked for Australian compliance before you commit, and we handle all certification and logistics to get it here.
