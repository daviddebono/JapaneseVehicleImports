Benefits & ROI

Why use a broker: compliance handled, team in Japan, bulk options for dealerships.

Compliance & Certification Handled

We ensure the vehicle can be imported to Australia and manage all compliance and certification required to get it here. You avoid the complexity of RAWS, approvals and customs yourself. We work with you so the vehicle lands ready for registration (subject to your state requirements).

Team on the Ground in Japan

Unlike many brokers, we have team members in Japan who inspect vehicles and purchase from auction on your behalf. That means better visibility over condition and price, and fewer surprises. It is a real efficiency and productivity advantage for both private buyers and dealerships.

Bulk Imports for Dealerships

Dealerships can give us a shopping list of desired cars. We confirm their eligibility for Australia and the quantity we can source, then manage the full import process. One point of contact, consistent documentation and a process that scales with your volume.

Efficiency for You

One broker from enquiry to delivery: eligibility check, sourcing (including auction), compliance, shipping and clearance. We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart. You focus on your business or enjoy your vehicle; we handle the rest.
