Japanese Truck Imports

Import Japanese light and medium trucks to Australia with full compliance and certification. Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart.

If you are looking for a broker to assist you in importing a truck from Japan, Japanese Vehicle Imports will ensure that the vehicle can be imported to Australia and we will manage all the compliance and certification to get it here. We service customers across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart, including major suburbs in each city. Whether you are a tradesperson, a fleet operator or a dealership, we confirm eligibility and manage the full import process.

Why Use a Broker for Japanese Truck Imports?

Japanese light and medium trucks are in strong demand in Australia for their build quality and value. Importing one requires eligibility verification, compliance with Australian design rules, certification (including through a RAWS-approved workshop where required), shipping and customs. As your broker we handle these steps so your truck lands ready for registration. We check each vehicle for Australian compliance before you commit, so you are not left with a truck that cannot be imported.

Our Team on the Ground in Japan

Unlike many brokers, we have members of our team on the ground in Japan. They inspect trucks and purchase them from auction on your behalf. That means you get real visibility over condition and price, and we can bid and buy with your authority. For trades and fleets this is a significant advantage: you know exactly what you are getting before the vehicle leaves Japan.

Bulk Truck Imports

We also do bulk vehicle imports. If you are a dealership or need multiple trucks, you can give us a shopping list. We confirm their eligibility for Australia and the quantity we can source, then manage the full process. Our team in Japan inspects and purchases from auction on your behalf at scale, so bulk orders are handled with the same rigour as single-vehicle imports.

What We Do From Start to Finish

From your first enquiry we confirm whether the truck you want can be imported to Australia. We then source the vehicle — including via Japanese auction where our team inspects and purchases on your behalf — and manage all compliance and certification. We coordinate shipping and customs clearance and deliver to your nominated location. You receive documentation for registration. For bulk orders we apply the same process at scale with consistent communication and paperwork.
