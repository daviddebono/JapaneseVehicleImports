Case Studies

Realistic scenarios showing how we work with buyers and dealerships. Clearly marked as illustrative.

Note: The following are illustrative scenarios only. Outcomes depend on vehicle type, eligibility and timing. We confirm eligibility and process when you enquire.

Single Car Import — Sydney

A private buyer in Sydney wanted a specific Japanese model. We confirmed Australian compliance, our team in Japan located and inspected the vehicle and purchased at auction. We managed compliance and certification and coordinated shipping to Sydney. The vehicle was delivered ready for registration.

Bulk Order for Dealership — Melbourne

A Melbourne dealership gave us a shopping list of desired cars. We confirmed eligibility and quantity, then our team in Japan sourced vehicles at auction, inspected and purchased on their behalf. We managed full compliance and certification and delivered to the nominated port. Consistent process and documentation for the whole order.

Van for Business — Brisbane

A Brisbane business needed a Japanese van for their fleet. We confirmed the model was compliant, sourced it (including auction option), and managed certification and delivery. They received the van with all paperwork for registration in Queensland.

Light Truck — Adelaide

A tradesperson in Adelaide wanted a light truck from Japan. We checked eligibility, our team in Japan found a suitable unit at auction and inspected it, and we handled compliance and shipping to Adelaide. Clear communication and documentation throughout.
