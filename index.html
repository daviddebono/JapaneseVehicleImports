How It Works

From enquiry to delivery: we confirm eligibility, manage compliance and certification, and coordinate shipping to Australia.

  1. Enquire — Tell us what you want (car, van or truck; single or bulk). We confirm it can be imported to Australia.
  2. Source — We source the vehicle (including via auction in Japan where our team inspects and purchases on your behalf).
  3. Compliance & Certification — We manage all compliance and certification required for Australian import.
  4. Ship & Clear — We coordinate shipping and customs clearance to your chosen port or location.
  5. Delivery — Your vehicle is delivered ready for registration (subject to your state requirements).

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you guarantee a vehicle can be imported to Australia?

We check each vehicle for Australian compliance before you commit. We manage all compliance and certification required to import it here. Eligibility depends on the specific model and Australian rules at the time.

Which areas do you service?

We service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart. We coordinate delivery to your chosen port or location; registration is handled according to your state requirements.

Do you buy from Japanese auctions?

Yes. Unlike many brokers, we have team members on the ground in Japan who inspect vehicles and purchase from auction on your behalf. This gives you better control and visibility over condition and price.

Do you do bulk imports for dealerships?

Yes. Dealerships can give us a shopping list of desired vehicles. We confirm their eligibility for Australia and the quantity we can source, then manage the full import process including compliance and certification.
