Services

Japanese car, van and truck imports to Australia. We manage compliance and certification and service Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart.

If you are looking for a broker to assist you in importing a vehicle from Japan, we ensure the vehicle can be imported to Australia and manage all compliance and certification to get it here. We also do bulk vehicle imports for dealerships — give us a shopping list and we confirm eligibility and quantity. Our team on the ground in Japan inspects and purchases from auction on your behalf.
